    The wife of Twilight star Jackson Rathbone posted an adorable photo of their son and he looks just like his dad.

    Sheila Hafsadi, Jackson’s wife, often posts pictures of their kids on Instagram and she comes up with charming, cute, and playful captions. This time, she shared a snap of five-year-old son, Monroe Jackson Rathbone VI.

    In the caption, the 31-year-old mom wrote:

    “Me: Hey Monroe…smile like daddy! Monroe: (see first pic above)”

    She then tagged Jackson and added several hashtags: #smile #park #playground #studiocity #likehisdaddy #thatfacetho #losangeles #blog #sixandboe #blogger #@jacksonrathbone

    Of course, Monroe smiled like his father—a smile that truly mirrors Jackson’s own smile. The followers agreed as well. Some of their comments read:

    “So cute haha (sic).”

    “That does seem like something Jackson would do. Lol.”

    "Aww, too cute lo. It’s amazing how kids really pay attention & catch on to everything.”

    “Yep! Those dimples!”

    Jackson and Sheila also have a daughter, Presley Bowie Rathbone, who is turning two years old later this month. The couple got married in 2013 after meeting each other when Jackson was touring with his music band, 100 Monkeys.
