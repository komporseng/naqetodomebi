    Bethenny Frankel Is Providing $20 Million Worth Of Emergency Supplies To Ukraine

    02 May 2024, 00:00

    Bethenny Frankel sends $20 million to Ukraine during Russia’s missile attacks. According to her statement to People magazine, the relief would consist of “100,000 crisis kits stocked with blankets, sleeping bags, amenity kits, non-perishables, and water, as well as generators.”

    BStrong, Frankel’s charity, collaborated with Global Empowerment Mission and originally intended to provide $10 million in relief. Nonetheless, the TV celebrity and entrepreneur stated on Friday that the target had been raised to $20 million. The philanthropist posted updates on the endeavor and footage of assistance boxes being packed from her organization’s warehouses on Instagram.

    According to BStrong’s official website,

    “Our new objective is to provide 20 m in assistance through 80 containers.” If there is a mass evacuation, we will aim for 100 containers, which we have done effectively in previous calamities. It’s a terrifying scenario.”
