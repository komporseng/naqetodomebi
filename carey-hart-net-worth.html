    Who is Carey Hart? His Career And Profession Details.

    Before getting into Carey Hart’s Net Worth, let’s find out about his career.

    Carey Jason Philip Hart is mostly known in the world of motocross as the first person to perform a backflip on a 250cc motorcycle. He is a retired motorcycle racer and freestyle motocross athlete. Currently, he is an off-road truck racer and a successful businessman.

    His career may have kick-started during his teenage years but had its origin at age 4. His father, Tom Hart, a construction company owner, gifted him with a motorcycle that soon became his passion. He competed in local races before turning pro at age 18. He has been accredited with several tricks, including the BMX Superman Seat Grab and The Hart Attack.

    Just like any other profession, his career has suffered a few setbacks. The most common being injuries sustained from the stunts he pulls. He tried to pull off the backflip two times and failed during the X-Games in the early 2000s. He only got it right in 2003 in Los Angeles.

    The athlete also opened up a tattoo shop called The Hart and Huntington Tattoo Company based in Vegas. It even got featured in the reality TV series Inked. He also formed his Supercross team back in 2007. Other achievements include the Hart and Huntington Off-road team and much more.
