    Casey Davidson Job: What Does Pete Davidson’s Sister Do For A Living?

    Casey Davidson is a graduate of Marist College. She is preparing to enter the medical field and finished her didactic year in August 2021. Since an individual who wishes to become a Physician’s Assistant (PA) takes the didactic year, it is presumed that she wishes to become a PA.

    In August 2021, Casey posted about completing her didactic year on her Instagram.

    According to the website of AAPA, PAs are medical professionals who diagnose illness, develop and manage treatment plans, prescribe medications, and often serve as a patient’s principal healthcare provider.

    Most of the time PA and Registered Nurse is often mistaken. Starting with the academic phase, one can become an RN if they either get an associate’s degree in Nursing or a Bachelor of Science in Nursing. Additionally, RNs need a minimum of two years of experience in healthcare coupled with a GPA of B or higher in the nursing program.

    Whereas, PAs have lesser choices but are required to obtain more education and training. Aspiring PAs need to have a master’s degree in physician assistant studies that usually lasts about two years. PA needs to be licensed with PANCE (Physician Assistant National Certifying Examination).

    Coming to their work, while registered nurses assist patients from a nursing approach, PAs assume a role more or less similar to that of physicians. PAs have a direct role in administering tests, performing examinations, and prescribing medication. They also assist in surgeries.

    Coming to the salary, RN makes nearly $75,500 per annually whereas Physician Assistants make $115,000 per year according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics.
