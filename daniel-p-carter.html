    Biography

    Daniel P Carter was born in England on November 16, 1972. Known for his work with the alternative rock bands A and Bloodhound Gang; the punk group Hexes; and the metal band Krokodil, this British guitarist, vocalist, and bassist also had a career as a disc jockey and radio host. His best-known singles include “Starbucks” and “Rush Song” (both with A). While on A’s 2005 Teen Dance Ordinance tour, he shared German tour dates with pop singer Avril Lavigne. He became a member of A in the late 1990s, hired as the replacement for the band’s former bassist, Stevie Swindon.
