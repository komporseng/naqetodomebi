    Eve Harlow: Acting Career, Movies, TV Shows

    Eve Harlow began appearing in modest roles in short films, feature films, and television shows in 2007. Her debut performance was in the Academy Award-winning picture Juno in 2007. Then She was cast as Tina Renwald in the Canadian television series The Guard in 2008. Harlow received a Leo Award in 2009 for Best Supporting Performance by a Female in a Dramatic Series for her performance, and she was nominated for a Gemini Award in 2010.

    Eve Harlow continued to work consistently on screen after that, appearing in films such as The Tall Man (2012) and television shows such as Flashpoint (2011) and Cracked (2013), among others. In 2012, she starred in Ashley McKenzie’s short film When You Sleep, which received multiple accolades and premiered at film festivals throughout the world, including the Cannes Film Festival.

    Harlow appeared as a guest star on many television episodes in 2014, including Fargo, Bitten, The Killing, and Lost Girl. That same year, she was cast as Maya Vie in the second season of The CW’s The 100.

    In 2015, Eve Harlow starred as Taylor Kravid in the NBC miniseries Heroes Reborn, based on the original series, and as Bethany, the roommate of the main character Rachel Goldberg, played by Shiri Appleby, in the Lifetime series UnREAL.

    In 2017, Harlow was cast as Tess, a recurring character in Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.‘s fifth season, and as Melody in the pilot for the upcoming courtroom drama The Jury. Harlow was cast as Ellen, a vicious assassin, in the Netflix series The Night Agent in 2023.
