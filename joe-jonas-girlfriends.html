    Joe Jonas caught the love bug several times during his time in the spotlight.

    Before marrying his now-estranged wife, Sophie Turner, the middle JoBro dated former Disney Channel co-stars, such as Demi Lovato and AJ Michalka. Rumors have circulated that Aly & AJ’s 2007 hit single “Potential Breakup Song” is actually about Joe. He later got a rep for breaking hearts of super stars, like Taylor Swift, when she revealed on Saturday Night Live that he “broke up with [her] over the phone.”

    After four years of marriage, Joe and Sophie shocked fans all over the world in September 2023 when they announced their split and impending divorce. The pair share two daughters together, whom they welcomed in 2020 and 2022, respectively. The former couple’s joint statement declared that their separation was a “united decision,” and the “Cake by the Ocean” artist filed for divorce from the Game of Thrones actress earlier that month.
