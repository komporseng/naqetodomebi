    Joseph Peter Pantoliano Wiki Biography

    Joseph Peter Pantoliano hails from Hoboken, New Jersey USA, and is an actor, known for his role as Ralph Cifaretto in the TV series “The Sopranos” (2000 – 2004). He has landed roles in various feature films like “Risky Business” (1983), “La Bamba” (1987), “The Matrix” (1999), and “Memento” (2000). He has been active in the entertainment industry since 1974.

    His net worth is estimated at $8 million, as of early 2017. Acting is the main source of Pantoliano’s wealth.

    Joe Pantoliano Net Worth $8 Million

    Pantoliano started his acting career in the mid-1970s with minor roles in various cinema and television productions. He appeared in the drama film “All My Stars” (1980) as one of his early significant roles. In the 1980s, he made guest appearances in several television series. He later starred in notable film productions including “Midnight Run” (1988), “The Fugitive” (1993), “Bound” (1996), “The Matrix” (1999), and “Memento” (2000), steadily increasing his net worth.

    Aside from films, Pantoliano also had a recurring role in the mafia series “The Sopranos” between 2000 and 2004, winning an Emmy Award. He made his directorial debut in 2003 with the film “Just Like Mona” and has been a film producer since the mid-1990s. He continued to star in various other films, augmenting his net worth further.

    He has also been involved in theatre, appearing in various plays. In 2003, he starred in the play “Frankie and Johnny in the Clair de Lune” on Broadway. Additionally, he has authored two autobiographical books – “Who’s Sorry Now: The True Story of a Stand-Up Guy” and “Asylum: Hollywood Tales From My Great Depression: Brain Dis-Ease, Recovery and Being My Mother’s Son”.
