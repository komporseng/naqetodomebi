    Ken Kostick Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

    Ken Kostick (1 June 1953 – 21 April 2011) was a Canadian chef and television and radio personality, best known for co-hosting the television series What's for Dinner? with Mary Jo Eustace.Kostick was born in Winnipeg and attended St. John's High School.He hosted He Said, She Said with Ken and Mary Jo on W Network. His other television credits included Ken Kostick and Company on Food Network Canada, Countertop to Table Cuisine on Global, and What's for Breakfast?, a morning radio show on Toronto, Ontario radio station CIRR-FM, from April 2007 to December 2008.He also wrote several bestselling cookbooks and put out an eponymous line of cooking products.On 21 April 2011, Kostick died in Toronto of complications of pancreatitis. He was 57 years old. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
