Furthermore, when the cops arrived, Dragun sprayed water on them as well as the hotel security employees. According to reports, Nikita was detained on a felony charge of assault against a police officer. The police also said that hotel personnel told them that Nikita had been generating a lot of ruckus in the hotel for quite some time.

    She also defied directions, according to the employees, while she was generating a commotion. Meanwhile, this has caused quite a commotion on social media, with many netizens posting some humorous replies to Dragun’s imprisonment.

    Nikita Dragun, a makeup artist and beauty expert, was born in 1996 and often shares lessons and beauty-related information on social media. She is well-known for producing several fashion films emphasizing different trends. She has a large following on Instagram and YouTube, with 9 million followers and 3.5 million subscribers, respectively.
