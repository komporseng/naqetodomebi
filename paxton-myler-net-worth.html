    Paxton Myler (b. September 6, 2008) is an aspiring American YouTuber, actor, gymnast, and martial artist best known as one of the stars of the family-friendly YouTube channel, Ninja Kidz TV, which features action skits, challenges, parodies, and other lifestyle content.

    Born in a family of martial art experts and gymnasts, he has been training martial arts from a fairly young age of three and is currently a 1st-degree black belt and a level 6 gymnast. The Utah native also runs his self-titled Youtube channel, Paxton Myler, where he posts vlogs, challenge videos, and action skits.

    Know About His Family Background, Twin Sister, Adopted Sibling

    Born to an actor, director, and martial art expert father, Shane Myler, and fifth-degree black belt mother, Alyson Myler, Paxton Myler has a twin sister named, Payton Myler. Likewise, he has two older siblings named, Bryton Myler and Ashton Myler as well as an adopted brother named, Kayson Myler.

    The twins are probably the most popular members of the group.
    Photo Source: Paxton Myler, Instagram

    Talking about his parents, they are the original creator of Ninja Kidz TV who managed and trained their kids to become ninjas. Besides, they are also the owner of their very own martial arts school called Pursuit of Mastery Martial Arts which provides karate and self-defense classes for people of any age group.

    Paxton Myler Net Worth: Know About His Income Source, Merchandise, and 'American Ninja Warrior Junior' Journey

    As of 2022, Paxton Myler is estimated to be worth $3 million as an actor, YouTube content creator, and social media influencer. Appearing in American Ninja Warrior Junior might have also added some to his fortune, but his earnings primarily come from his YouTube videos.

    Talking about his collaborative YouTube channel, Ninja Kidz TV was launched in Feb 2017 and has over 18 million subscribers as of April 2022. Their first-ever video, POWER RANGERS MOVIE KIDZ TEASER, which was posted one month after the launch of their YouTube channel has just about 8 million views at the time of writing.

    With over 9 billion total views and 260 videos, they make an average of around $70,000 per video alone from ad revenue, those with over 30 million views at least (and there are plenty more videos easily hitting over 100 million views). One of the most popular videos on their channel is titled Paxton and Payton Twin Smash!! Super Team Up! which received over 391 million views and 1.5 million likes.

    Somehow, the kids have been consistently generating millions of views in their videos.

Some of their videos are sponsored by different toy brands like Mattel, Moose Toys, OSMO super studio, and so on.
